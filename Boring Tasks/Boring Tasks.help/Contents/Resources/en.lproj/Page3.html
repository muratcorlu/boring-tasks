Task Items

Task Items are main items to track and manage your tasks. After selection a Task List from left menu, you can create a task with the “Add Item” button bottom right.

You will asked for a title and period. Task Period is the time range that you will need to do this task regularly.

After adding your tasks, your list will be shown with the order of due dates. You can right click and click “Done” when you done that task. Of if you want to skip a task for one period, you click “skip” on context menu. When you set done or skip, due date will be updated with next period from now.

Blue and gray dots below tasks indicates history of your done and skip for the task.
